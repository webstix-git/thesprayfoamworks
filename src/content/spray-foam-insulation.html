What It Is

The best air seal money can buy

Spray polyurethane foam is applied as a liquid that expands on contact, filling every gap, crack, and cavity before hardening into a continuous barrier. Unlike batts, it doesn't sag, settle, or leave gaps, so your building stops leaking conditioned air.

That single step, sealing and insulating at once, is why foam outperforms every traditional material on comfort, efficiency, and moisture control.

✓Air-tight barrier, stops drafts and air infiltration cold.
✓Higher R-value per inch than fiberglass or cellulose.
✓Adds rigidity and blocks moisture in the assembly.
